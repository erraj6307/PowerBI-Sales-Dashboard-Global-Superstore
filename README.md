# 📊 Sales Dashboard using Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project showcases an **interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard** built using the Global Superstore dataset.
The objective is to analyze sales performance, identify trends, and deliver actionable insights for better business decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** → Dashboard & Visualization
* **Power Query** → Data Cleaning & Transformation (ETL)
* **DAX** → Calculated Measures & Columns
* **Microsoft Excel** → Data Source

## 📈 Key Insights

* 💰 **Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales**, making it the primary revenue driver
* 🌍 **North America and Europe dominate global sales performance**, indicating strong market presence
* 🏆 **Top customers generate a significant portion of total revenue**, showing dependency on key accounts
* 📦 **Few products drive most of the profit**, while several products consistently generate losses
* ⚠️ **Loss-making products require pricing or cost optimization strategies**
* 🔁 **Returned orders negatively impact profitability**, highlighting the need for better quality control
* 🚚 **Delivery performance is consistent**, but optimizing delivery time can further improve customer satisfaction

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on high-performing regions and customer segments
* Promote top profit-generating products
* Re-evaluate pricing strategy for loss-making items
* Improve logistics to reduce delivery time and returns
* Strengthen retention strategies for high-value customers
